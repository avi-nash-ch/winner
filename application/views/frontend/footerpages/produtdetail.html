Zoom! is a three-year integrated Early Years Education series for pre-primary levels in the school curriculum. The series caters to the age group of 3 to 6 years for the Pre-Primary, Lower Kindergarten, and Upper Kindergarten classes. It amalgamates the essence of Montessori education with the features of the National Education Policy for the linguistic, motor, and cognitive milestones of the early years of a child’s life. The series consists of three kits containing books and learning materials, a robotic coding game, and an app for learning at home.
